More about Orthopedics

Arthroscopic and robotic surgery is widely used in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases and injuries occurring within the joint. Arthroscopy is done with a few minor skin incisions and causes a very small scar and pain. A rapid recovery is observed compared to open surgery. The operation takes approximately 1 hour and the patient is discharged a few hours after the operation. The stiches do not need to be removed as they are done with fusible material. In a wide range of patients – from professional athletes to housewives – knee-shoulder-hand and ankle arthroscopy, ligament cartilage-meniscus-tendon operations, cartilage transplants are successfully performed by Turkish orthopedists at a time to suit you.

Most frequent orthopedic procedures:

  • Joints replacement
  • Intervertebral hernia treatment
  • Bone tumors removal
  • Microsurgery
  • Rehabilitation
